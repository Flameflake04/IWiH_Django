About This Project

The Invisible Woman in Healthcare project is dedicated to addressing gender bias in medical research. Despite advancements in healthcare, women continue to be underrepresented in clinical studies, research authorship, and medical trials. My project uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze and highlight women's underrepresentation in healthcare research papers.
I evaluate research papers based on:
1. Pronoun frequency analysis to identify biases in language usage.
2. Author gender distribution in references to evaluate representation in academic contributions.
3. Participant demographics in studies to determine inclusivity in medical trials.
